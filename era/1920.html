When the "i" icon is tapped, an information window is shown. This window gives a brief description of how dating was viewed and pursued in that particular era. There are also a set of rules for participants to follow in order to maintain the most authentic experience possible.

When a man is viewing a woman's profile, he will see the woman's profile picture, name, and age. He can also tap the "i" icon to view her date preferences. This era is all about wining and dining. He can swipe left or tap the "x" icon if he is not interested. Or he can swipe right or tap the "heart" icon if he wants to chat with her.

In this era, woman are able to view and select matches. When a woman is viewing a man's profile, she will see the man's profile picture, name, age, occupation and salary. Men with the highest salary will appear first in a woman's potential matches. She can also tap the "i" icon to view his ideas for a first date. She can swipe left or tap the "x" icon if she is not interested. Or she can swipe right or tap the "heart" icon if she wishes to chat with him.

Both men and women are notified if they have a match. However, the woman must message first. She may hit the forward arrow to proceed to the chat screen. Both men and women can select the back arrow to continue looking through potential matches.

The chat screen is relatively standard to most mobile dating apps on the market today.
